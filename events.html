Events

We hold service every Sunday, excluding the 5th Sunday. We begin with Intercessory Prayer at 9:30am followed by Bible Church School at 9:45am and Morning Worship at 11:15am.

Bible Study is held every Wednesday at 7:30pm. It is preceded by Intercessory Prayer which begins at 7:00pm.

Due to COVID-19 all events have been cancelled. Please feel free to join us for service every Sunday via Facebook.
